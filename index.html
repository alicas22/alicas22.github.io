About Me

As a Full Stack Developer and Software Engineer with a background in both software engineering and mortgage sales, I am passionate about creating innovative solutions to help organizations achieve their technical goals. Currently, I work for Sojourn Creations as a Full Stack Engineer, focusing on generative AI gameplay, socket communications, and backend models and routing.

My journey into software began after completing a BS in Biomedical Engineering, and since then, I’ve developed proficiency in a wide range of programming languages and technologies, including Python, JavaScript, and C#.

I have experience working with both frontend and backend technologies, building full-stack applications using React, Next.js, Django, Node.js, and Express. My expertise extends to databases such as PostgreSQL, MS SQL Server, and Firebase, as well as utilizing APIs, WebSockets, and third-party integrations like OpenAI and Stripe.

I have consistently demonstrated my ability to optimize performance and streamline processes in diverse software projects, including improving database query efficiency and enhancing real-time multiplayer experiences. Whether developing AI-driven flashcard applications or boba shop recommendation engines, I’m committed to building cutting-edge software solutions that make an impact.
